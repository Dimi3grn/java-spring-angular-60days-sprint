Héritage, polymorphisme (extends, super, @Override)
Interfaces (implements, couplage faible)
Collections (List, Map)
Exceptions (try-catch, custom exceptions)
static, final, abstract

@Service, architecture en couches (Controller → Service → Repository)
Validation (@Valid, @NotNull, @Size)

CORS entre Spring Boot et Angular
